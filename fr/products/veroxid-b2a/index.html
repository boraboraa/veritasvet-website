VerOxid B2A

Synthetic Antioxidants — Preserve Your Feed for Optimal Animal Nutrition

VEROXID B2A combines Butylated Hydroxytoluene (BHT) and Butylated Hydroxyanisole (BHA), synthetic antioxidants specially formulated to enhance the shelf life and quality of animal feeds. These powerful ingredients prevent oxidative degradation, ensuring your feed stays fresh and nutritious.

Avantages Clés

  • Prevents Rancidity — protects fats and oils from oxidation maintaining palatability and nutrient availability
  • Enhances Nutrient Stability — keeps vitamins and sensitive ingredients effective for longer periods
  • Extends Shelf Life — prolongs storage duration of complete feeds and feed ingredients
  • Improves Qualité de l'Aliment — supports livestock health and consistent performance

Applications

Suitable for ruminants, monogastrics and other livestock. Ideal for premix formulations. Also suitable for pet food.
