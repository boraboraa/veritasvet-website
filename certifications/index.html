Certified Quality. Proven European Standards.

At VeritasVet, quality and compliance are fundamental to our operations. Our products are manufactured under internationally recognized certification systems, ensuring safety, traceability, and regulatory compliance across European and global markets.

GMP

With GMP certification, we demonstrate that our products are manufactured under controlled and standardized conditions, ensuring consistent quality and safety at every production stage.

HACCP & SGS

HACCP and SGS certifications confirm that our processes are systematically controlled and independently audited according to international safety and quality standards.

Halal

Halal certification demonstrates that our products and manufacturing processes comply with recognized Halal standards for international markets.

A-Futter

A-Futter certification confirms compliance with recognized feed production and safety standards.

QS

QS certification proves adherence to strict European requirements for quality assurance and full supply chain traceability.

VLOG

VLOG certification confirms compliance with verified non-GMO sourcing and production standards.

Our Commitment to Continuous Compliance

All certifications are subject to regular audits and continuous monitoring to ensure sustained compliance with evolving European and international regulations.
