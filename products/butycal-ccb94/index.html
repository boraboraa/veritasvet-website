ButyCal CCB94

Encapsulated Calcium Butyrate — Enhancing Intestinal Integrity for Optimal Performance

BUTYCAL is a premium encapsulated calcium butyrate designed specifically for livestock. Butyrate is a short-chain fatty acid that plays a crucial role in maintaining gut health and integrity. By protecting the butyrate in an encapsulated form, BUTYCAL ensures targeted delivery within the gastrointestinal tract, maximizing its benefits.

Key Benefits

  • Improved Intestinal Integrity — supports gut cell health reducing permeability and promoting a robust intestinal barrier
  • Enhanced Nutrient Absorption — helps optimize nutrient absorption for better overall health and performance
  • Supports Immune Function — contributes to balanced gut microbiota enhancing immune response
  • Promotes Growth Performance — aids in improving feed efficiency and livestock growth metrics

Applications

Suitable for ruminants, monogastrics, poultry and swine. Ideal during stress periods such as weaning and transport.
