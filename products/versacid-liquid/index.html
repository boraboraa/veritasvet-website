VersAcid Liquid

Premium Multi-Acid Acidifier — Optimizing Drinking Water for Healthier Animals

VerAcid Liquid is a premium multi-acid acidifier formulated with a unique blend of formic acid, propionic acid, phosphoric acid, and citric acid. Designed specifically for use in the drinking water of livestock, VerAcid Liquid enhances water quality, promotes digestive health, prevents pathogen growth and contributes to overall animal health.

Key Benefits

  • Enhanced Acidification — combination of four acids lowers drinking water pH more effectively than any single acid
  • Broad Spectrum Antimicrobial Action — multi-faceted approach against bacteria yeast and molds
  • Improved Gut Health — promotes optimal pH environment supporting beneficial bacteria
  • Effective Nutrient Utilization — improves water quality and feed conversion efficiency
  • Comprehensive Mineral Management — phosphoric acid reduces effects of hard water
  • Stress Reduction and Immune Support — contributes to stronger immune responses and greater animal resilience
