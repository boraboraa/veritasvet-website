Our Role in the Industry

VeritasVet operates as an independent formulation and application specialist.

Rather than promoting a fixed catalog, we select and combine technologies from qualified manufacturing partners to deliver the most appropriate solution for each situation.

This flexible approach allows us to focus on performance, reliability and cost-efficiency — ensuring solutions match field realities, raw-material variability and production objectives across livestock, poultry and aquaculture systems.

Proven Performance

Our solutions are assessed through recognized validation systems. Mycotoxin management technologies are evaluated using the UGent simulated gastrointestinal digestion model and independently verified by Trilogy Analytical Laboratories (USA).

Our collaboration model is supported by manufacturing partners operating under internationally recognized certification systems.

All products comply with GMP+, HACCP, FAMI-QS and ISO 22000 standards.
