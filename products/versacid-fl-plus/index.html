VersAcid FL Plus

Synergistic Fatty Acid Solution — Enhancing Animal Health and Performance

VERSACID FL Plus is a premium formulation combining short-chain fatty acids (formic acid, lactic acid, citric acid) with medium-chain fatty acids (capric acid and caprylic acid). This unique blend supports animal health, improves digestion, and enhances overall performance in livestock and poultry.

Key Benefits

  • Enhanced Gut Health — promotes balanced gut microbiota by inhibiting harmful bacteria
  • Improved Nutrient Absorption — increases feed digestibility for better nutrient utilization
  • Natural Antimicrobial Properties — formic and lactic acids reduce pathogens within the gastrointestinal tract
  • Boosts Immune Response — natural components contribute to greater resilience against diseases

How It Works

  • Formic Acid — strong antimicrobial inhibits harmful bacteria yeasts and molds
  • Lactic Acid — maintains healthy pH for optimal digestion
  • Citric Acid — natural preservative contributing to formulation stability
  • Capric and Caprylic Acids — strong antimicrobial effects and energy source promoting beneficial bacteria

Dosage

1–3 kg per ton of feed. Suitable for ruminants poultry and swine.
