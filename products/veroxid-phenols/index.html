VerOxid Phenols

Natural Antioxidant Solution — Harnessing the Power of Polyphenols for Optimal Health

VEROXID Phenols is a dry blend of carefully selected natural extracts rich in polyphenols, designed to provide potent antioxidant protection for animals. Polyphenols neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress, contributing to overall health and performance. This powerful formulation acts as a natural replacer for Vitamin E in animal diets.

Key Benefits

  • Powerful Antioxidant — high polyphenol content combats oxidative stress and cellular damage
  • Natural Vitamin E Replacer — provides similar protective effect from natural sources
  • Synergistic Effect — diverse natural extracts maximize combined antioxidant potential
  • Supports Immune Function — strengthens immune system helping animals resist diseases
  • Enhances Performance — improves feed efficiency growth rates and reproductive health
